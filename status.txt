Zenrush — Working Context

Game
Top-down auto-attack shooter roguelite for iOS/Android. 10-minute hard-capped runs, mid-run upgrades every 60 seconds, persistent shard-based meta progression. Minimalist monochromatic visuals inspired by Nodebuster. References: Archero, Survivor.io, Halls of Torment, Brotato.
Core loop: fight escalating waves → collect shards → pick mid-run upgrades → bank shards → spend on permanent meta upgrades → repeat.

Code Standards (non-negotiable)

No magic numbers — everything exported or derived
No hardcoded BS — use engine-native solutions where possible
No over-engineering or unjustified singletons
Clean separation of responsibilities
Composition over inheritance
Senior-level solutions only — offer options before attempting anything uncertain or smelly
Push back when something is wrong, don't take the easy way out
